Education

Double degree | Electronique et Informatique Industrielle | 2018 - 2020

I always wanted to do a study exchange program in the university, know a different culture and develop myself with this experience (professional and personal aspects). I had the opportunity to participate in a double degree program at INSA Rennes (France), and this was a very enriching experience. At INSA I studied Electronics and Computer Engineering, a course with a great focus on computing. However, besides a great education, I believe that my greatest developments were living in a different culture and country, learning French in a short period of time, and meeting different people and places. I like to say that this two-year experience expanded my vision of the world.

Control and Automation Engineering | 2015 - currently

The first time I visited Unicamp was in 2014, and I was delighted with the interdisciplinary and multicultural environment. I chose to study Control and Automation Engineering because this course included knowledge of the areas of mechanics, electrical and informatics, and I was willing to have a broader view of engineering. During my time at university I participated in several extracurricular activities, the junior enterprise of my course (Mecatron), an organizing committee for events of the junior enterprise movement (InterAção), the robotics team of Unicamp (Phoenix) and the organizing team of the academic week of my course (Automática).
